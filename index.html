Welcome to play chess game

  1. The fundamental rules of chess include alternating moves (White moves first),
  2. each piece having unique movement, capturing by landing on a square,
  3. the goal to checkmate the opponent's king (not capture it),
  4. special moves like Pawn promotion, castling, and en passant.
  5. Key strategic principles for success are controlling the center,
  6. developing minor pieces (knights and bishops) early,
  7. keeping the king safe by castling,
  8. coordinating attacks to overwhelm the opponent's defenses.
